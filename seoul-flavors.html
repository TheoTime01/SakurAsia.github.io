About the tour

Seoul Flavors is a compact, taste-forward itinerary blending markets, a hands-on cooking class, palace heritage, and a serene tea ceremony. You’ll stay in a boutique hotel and explore with a food-loving local guide—unhurried and delicious.

Planned itinerary

  1. Day 1 — Arrive in Seoul (ICN/GMP)

    Meet & greet transfer. Settle into boutique hotel near Insadong. Evening stroll and welcome dinner.

  2. Day 2 — Palaces, Bukchon & Tea

    Gyeongbokgung exterior grounds (as available), Bukchon hanok lanes, traditional tea ceremony, gallery browsing.

  3. Day 3 — Gwangjang Market & Cooking Class

    Market tastings (bindaetteok, mayak kimbap), hands-on cooking with a chef, optional Namsan sunset.

  4. Day 4 — Free Morning & Departure

    Leisurely breakfast and café time. Airport transfer for outbound flight.

Note: order and activities may adjust due to season, logistics, and local availability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you adapt the food experiences for my diet?

Yes—vegetarian, halal, gluten-free, and allergy-aware options are available. Tell us your needs on enquiry.

Is the cooking class suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. It’s hands-on, guided by a friendly chef, and perfect for all levels.

Can I add a day trip to Suwon or DMZ?

Yes, we can extend the tour and add extra day trips or hotel nights on request.
